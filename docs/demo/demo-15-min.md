# 15-Minute Demo

Date: 2026-03-11
Audience: Buyer, sponsor, or executive preview

## Demo objective

Show that the product is a B2B enterprise workspace platform with a governed workflow chain and credible enterprise control-plane surfaces.

## Opening framing

Say this clearly:

- This is a B2B enterprise workspace platform, not a consumer app.
- The current foundation is OA v1 with nine roles.
- Okta OIDC is the only enterprise login target in scope.
- `local_lab` is the sandbox / preview workspace.

## Suggested flow

1. Open Workspace Overview.
   - Show the enterprise workspace cockpit.
   - Point out the role-aware governed-flow panel.
2. Walk the main chain:
   - Request Center
   - Approval Center
   - Operations Console
   - Review Center
   - Audit & Reporting Center
   - Keep the same task in focus.
3. Open Members & Access.
   - Show seat scope, invite lifecycle, join trace, and access boundaries.
4. Open Integration & Readiness Center.
   - Show Okta OIDC-only readiness framing.
5. Close in Policy & Governance Center or Audit & Reporting Center.
   - Show policy basis or export bundle framing.

## Key talking points

- Same task, same evidence set, different role-aware views
- One workspace shell, not disconnected admin pages
- Explicit blocker and next-action posture
- Honest sandbox framing without fake production claims

## What not to say

- Do not say production-ready
- Do not say full pilot complete
- Do not imply multiple IdP providers
- Do not describe `local_lab` as a live tenant
