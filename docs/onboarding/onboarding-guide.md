# Onboarding Guide

Date: 2026-03-11
Audience: New internal operator, admin, demo host, or field engineer

## Purpose

This guide helps a new team member onboard to the current governed enterprise workspace platform without overclaiming scope or maturity.

## Core framing

Start every onboarding with these statements:

- The B-end product name is `Lumio`.
- The product is B2B only.
- The current identity target is Okta OIDC only.
- The current role foundation is OA v1 with nine roles.
- `local_lab` is a sandbox / preview workspace.
- The current package does not claim full real-pilot closure or full production completeness.

## Minimum product tour

1. Workspace Overview
2. Request Center
3. Approval Center
4. Operations Console
5. Review Center
6. Audit & Reporting Center
7. Members & Access
8. Integration & Readiness Center
9. Policy & Governance Center
10. Organization & Workspace

## What each audience usually cares about

- Buyer / sponsor:
  - workspace cockpit
  - governed workflow story
  - admin credibility
- Admin:
  - members/access
  - readiness
  - policy
  - escalation path
- Audit / governance:
  - evidence continuity
  - receipt timeline
  - export bundle framing

## Demo-safe language

Use:

- preview
- sandbox
- current foundation
- current governed workspace implementation
- buyer-facing preview package

Avoid:

- production-ready
- full pilot complete
- all enterprise rollout blockers solved
- multiple IdP support

## Suggested hands-on walkthrough

1. Open the workspace in `local_lab`.
2. Keep one trial task selected.
3. Walk the governed chain.
4. Switch roles and show the same task from another role lens.
5. Show Members & Access, Readiness, Policy, and Audit.

## Expected outcome

The new team member should be able to:

- explain what the product is
- explain what it is not
- run the short demo
- answer the most common scope questions honestly
