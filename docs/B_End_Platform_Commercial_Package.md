# B-End Platform Commercial Package

Date: 2026-03-10
Owner: Codex
Scope: B-end platform only

## One-line positioning
Lumi is a B-end enterprise execution platform that turns governed work into visible, reviewable, role-based operational flow.

## Who it is for
- Enterprise requester teams
- Operations teams
- Review / approval owners
- Tenant and workspace admins
- Audit-facing stakeholders

## Core modules
- Request Center
- Approval Center
- Review Center
- Operations Console
- Integration & Readiness Center
- Policy & Governance Center
- Audit & Reporting Center

## Standard demo path
1. Workspace Overview
2. Organization & Workspace
3. Members & Access
4. Approval Center
5. Review Center
6. Audit & Reporting Center
7. Integration & Readiness Center

## Deployment offering
- Web-first B-end platform
- Enterprise session and role model
- Postgres-backed authoritative enterprise write path
- Explicit fail-closed posture when production persistence is not ready

## Pilot-to-paid narrative
- Start with a bounded B-end evaluation flow
- validate membership, role, approval, review, and audit operations
- confirm deployment and persistence posture
- expand from evaluation to controlled enterprise rollout
