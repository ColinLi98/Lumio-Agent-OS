# Lumio 15-Minute Demo Talk Track

Date: 2026-03-11
Audience: buyer intro, executive preview, internal review
Goal: show a governed enterprise workspace preview with one clear buyer narrative

## Demo framing

Open with these exact points:

- Lumio is a governed enterprise workspace preview.
- It is an enterprise workspace platform, not a consumer product.
- The product backbone is the OA v1 nine-role model.
- Okta OIDC is the only enterprise login target in scope.
- `current workspace` is the path being deepened into the more credible enterprise path.
- `local_lab` is a sandbox / preview workspace.
- This demo does not claim full production closure or full pilot closure.

## Demo path

### Minute 0-2: Positioning

Say:

- The product is one enterprise workspace shell.
- The main governed flow is `Request -> Approval -> Operations -> Review -> Audit`.
- The control plane is inside the same workspace, not a detached admin product.

### Minute 2-5: Workspace Overview

Show:

- Workspace Overview
- role-aware cockpit
- next action
- blocker posture
- governed flow continuity on one task

Say:

- The same task can be understood across multiple role lenses without changing task identity.
- This is what makes the workspace shell more credible than a section-by-section demo.

### Minute 5-8: Governed Flow

Move through:

1. Request Center
2. Approval Center
3. Operations Console
4. Review Center
5. Audit & Reporting Center

Call out:

- current stage
- waiting-on role
- blocker
- audit receipt
- evidence bundle

### Minute 8-11: Control Plane

Open:

- Members & Access
- Integration & Readiness Center
- Policy & Governance Center

Show:

- invite lifecycle and join trace
- readiness checklist and next action
- policy basis and decision rationale

Say:

- This is not just a workflow demo. The control plane explains why the governed flow can or cannot move.

### Minute 11-13: Audit

Open:

- Audit & Reporting Center

Show:

- audit receipt
- traceability
- evidence bundle continuity
- export boundary

Say:

- Audit export is preview-only framing today. We show the audit receipt and evidence bundle honestly without claiming a full production export program.

### Minute 13-15: Close

Close with:

- today’s value: governed flow + control plane + role-aware enterprise shell
- current maturity: credible buyer-facing preview
- current limit: not full production closure, not full pilot closure, Okta OIDC only

## Questions to expect

- Why only Okta OIDC?
- What is the difference between `current workspace` and `local_lab`?
- What is already real versus still preview?

## What not to say

- do not say “production-ready”
- do not say “pilot already closed”
- do not imply provider breadth beyond Okta OIDC
- do not describe `local_lab` as a live tenant
